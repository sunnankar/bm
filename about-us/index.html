Bitcoin Mining is a website dedicated to making Bitcoin mining easier to understand and more accessible for beginners. We strive to provide the most updated information about Bitcoin mining while making it easier for beginners to start mining.

It is managed by a small team that includes many volunteers from across the Bitcoin mining community who submit Github pull requests.

History

Bitcoin Mining started in 2010 when we recognized that the growing interest in Bitcoins also contributed to the growing interest in Bitcoin mining.
